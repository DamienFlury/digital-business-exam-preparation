# Mission: Pass the DigiBusI Modulschlussprüfung (MSP)

## Why
Damien sits the DigiBusI (Digital Business I, OST) module-final exam on **25 June 2026, 09:50–11:50**. The goal is concrete: walk in able to answer open + multiple-choice questions across all 11 lecture topics confidently, and pass — ideally comfortably.

## Success looks like
- Can write full-sentence open answers (the exam expects prose, not keywords) for any of the 11 topics.
- Can correctly fill the categorisation / true-false tables that recur in the exam AND justify each choice in writing.
- Has the recurring frameworks memorised cold (closed-book, no aids): 4 dimensions of digital transformation, Business Model Canvas elements, EDI standards, sell/buy/marketplace e-procurement, analytical vs operational CRM, content-based vs collaborative filtering, M2M payment, etc.
- Can produce a concrete real-world example on demand for almost any concept (the exam rewards examples heavily — often 2 of 3 points).

## Constraints
- **Hard deadline: 25 June 2026.** ~11 days from first session (14 June).
- Closed book — everything must be recalled from memory.
- ~120 points, ~1 min/point. Time management matters; answers must be efficient.
- All 11 topics weighted roughly equally — no topic can be skipped.
- Course materials are German; exam answers in German. Lectures mix German/English terminology.

## Out of scope
- The guest lecture ("Gastvortrag") under topic 1 is explicitly excluded from the exam.
- Going deeper than exam level on any single topic — breadth across all 11 beats depth in one.
